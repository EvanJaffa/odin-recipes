Comfort Food Pasta Recipe

Description

Everyone loves some standard boxed pasta made like a comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • Boxed pasta (any variety) - 1 box
  • Grated parmesean cheese (I love the green can Kraft variety) - 2+ tbsp
  • Butter - 1+ tbsp
  • Chicken or sausage, if desired
  • Sauce of choice, if desired

Steps

  1. Boil water according to pasta box instructions
  2. Begin cooking chicken, sausage, or other meat as desired.
  3. Once water is boiling, add pasta. Cook uncovered for time specified on pasta box instructions.
  4. Melt or soften butter in a pan or microwave.
  5. After meat is cooked, dice up into small chunks to be mixed with pasta.
  6. After pasta is cooked, drain and place into a large mixing bowl.
  7. Add diced meat if applicable, and stir in melted butter and parmesean cheese until pasta is evenly coated.
  8. Serve!
