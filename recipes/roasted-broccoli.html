Roasted Broccoli Recipe

Description

Roasting vegetables like broccoli or cauliflower is a great way to make them taste much better while remaining healthy.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli or cauliflower (or both!)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic

Steps

  1. If broccoli is frozen, thaw
  2. Begin cooking chicken, sausage, or other meat as desired.
  3. Once water is boiling, add pasta. Cook uncovered for time specified on pasta box instructions.
  4. Melt or soften butter in a pan or microwave.
  5. Serve!
