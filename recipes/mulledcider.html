Mulled Cider Recipe

Description

Mulled cider (also sometimes referred to as "hot cider"/"mulled wine" or other monikers) is a great drink for fall! This recipe can easily be multiplied to serve as many poeple as needed.

Ingredients

  • Red wine (sweet or dry, for taste) (1 bottle)
  • Apple cider (1/2 gallon)
  • 1 orange (sliced)
  • Brandy or honeyed whisky
  • Cinnamon
  • Other spices (cloves, anise, nutmeg, etc.)

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup of apple cider and 1 cup of wine. Heat until warm.
  2. Taste the prepared mix of cider and wine. Adjust as necessary to personal taste, then add remaining cider and wine.
  3. Slice orange and add to mix.
  4. Add spices. Stir regularly until hot.
  5. Serve!
